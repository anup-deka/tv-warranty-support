SECTION 1: WARRANTY OVERVIEW

VistaTech Electronics ("VistaTech", "we", "us") provides a limited warranty
on all TV products manufactured and sold under the VistaClear, NovaPic, and
LumaBright brands. This warranty is extended to the original end-user
purchaser ("Customer") and is non-transferable.

This warranty covers manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship.
It does not cover damage resulting from accident, misuse, unauthorized
modification, or normal wear and tear.

SECTION 2: WARRANTY COVERAGE PERIODS

2.1 Standard Warranty
All VistaTech TV models come with a 1-year Standard Warranty from the date
of purchase. This covers:
  • Panel defects (dead pixels exceeding 5 pixels in a 1cm² area)
  • Internal component failure under normal use conditions
  • Backlight failure not caused by physical damage
  • Factory software defects present at time of purchase

2.2 Extended Warranty
Customers who purchase an Extended Warranty plan (available within 30 days
of original purchase) receive 3 years of additional coverage beyond the
Standard Warranty, for a total of 4 years from the purchase date. Extended
Warranty additionally covers:
  • One free screen replacement due to manufacturing defects
  • Priority support with a 24-hour response time
  • Free on-site technician visit (once per warranty term)

2.3 Premium Warranty
The Premium Warranty plan provides 5 years of total coverage from the date
of purchase and includes all Extended Warranty benefits plus:
  • Coverage for accidental physical damage (one claim per year)
  • Advanced replacement service: a replacement unit is shipped before
    the defective unit is returned
  • Dedicated premium support hotline with no wait time
  • Free annual calibration service

2.4 Panel Size Warranty Addendum
TVs with screens 65 inches or larger receive an additional 6-month warranty
extension on the display panel regardless of warranty tier, in recognition
of the higher mechanical stress on large-format displays.

SECTION 3: WHAT IS COVERED

Under all warranty tiers, VistaTech warrants the following:

3.1 Display Panel
  • Dead pixels: Covered if 5 or more permanently non-functioning pixels
    are present in any 1cm² area of the screen
  • Bright or dark spots caused by internal LCD/OLED layer failure
  • Vertical or horizontal lines running across the display due to
    internal panel component failure

3.2 Electronics and Circuitry
  • Main board failure causing no-power or no-picture conditions
  • Power supply unit (PSU) failure not caused by electrical surges
  • HDMI port failure (input/output board defects)
  • USB port failure from internal circuit defects (not physical damage)
  • Speaker and audio system defects present within the first 90 days
  • Remote control defects during the first 90 days of ownership

3.3 Software and Firmware
  • Factory firmware bugs that prevent core TV functions (channel tuning,
    app launching, menu navigation)
  • VistaTech will issue software updates free of charge throughout the
    warranty period to resolve known firmware issues
  • Smart TV operating system failures caused by internal software faults

SECTION 4: WHAT IS NOT COVERED

The following are explicitly excluded from all warranty coverage:

4.1 Physical Damage
  • Cracked, broken, or shattered screens from drops, impacts, or pressure
  • Dents, scratches, or damage to the TV chassis or stand
  • Damage from falling objects or spills
  • Bent or broken HDMI/USB ports due to force

4.2 Environmental and Electrical Damage
  • Damage caused by power surges, lightning strikes, or unstable voltage
    (use of a surge protector is strongly recommended)
  • Damage from extreme heat, cold, humidity, or exposure to direct sunlight
  • Burn-in caused by displaying static images for extended periods
    (Note: OLED image retention after normal use is not covered under
    Standard Warranty but is covered under Extended and Premium plans
    if it occurs within the first 2 years)
  • Water or liquid damage of any kind

4.3 User and Third-Party Modifications
  • Repairs or modifications performed by unauthorized service centers
  • Damage caused by use of non-approved accessories or cables
  • Software modifications, sideloading of unauthorized apps, or
    rooting/jailbreaking the TV operating system
  • Use of the TV for commercial or rental purposes

4.4 Cosmetic and Consumable Items
  • Normal fading or discoloration of plastic components over time
  • Scratches on the screen due to cleaning or contact
  • Remote control batteries
  • Cleaning kits and accessories

SECTION 5: HOW TO MAKE A WARRANTY CLAIM

5.1 Before You File a Claim
Before initiating a warranty claim, customers should:
  1. Verify your TV is registered and the warranty is still active. You
     can check your warranty status by entering your serial code at
     support.vistatech.com or calling 1-800-VISTA-TV.
  2. Attempt basic troubleshooting steps as outlined in your TV's
     user manual or on our support website.
  3. Have your proof of purchase ready (receipt or order confirmation).

5.2 Filing a Claim
To file a warranty claim:
  • Online: Visit support.vistatech.com/claims and complete the claim form
  • Phone: Call 1-800-VISTA-TV (Mon–Fri 8AM–8PM, Sat 9AM–5PM EST)
  • In-person: Visit any authorized VistaTech service center

When filing, you will need:
  • Your TV's serial number (found on the label on the back of the TV
    or in Settings > About > Serial Number)
  • Proof of purchase showing the date of sale
  • A description of the issue and, if possible, photos or video

5.3 Claim Review Process
  1. Initial Review (1–2 business days): A support agent reviews your
     submission and verifies warranty eligibility.
  2. Diagnostic Assessment (2–5 business days): A technician may contact
     you to perform remote diagnostics or schedule an in-home visit.
  3. Resolution Decision: Based on the diagnosis, we will either:
     (a) Authorize a repair at an authorized service center
     (b) Arrange an in-home repair (Extended/Premium plans)
     (c) Issue a replacement unit
     (d) Offer a prorated refund if repair/replacement is not feasible

5.4 Turnaround Times
  • Standard Warranty repairs: 7–14 business days after receiving the unit
  • Extended Warranty on-site repairs: 3–7 business days after approval
  • Premium Warranty advanced replacement: Replacement unit ships within
    1 business day of claim approval

SECTION 6: REPAIR AND REPLACEMENT POLICY

6.1 Authorized Service Centers
All warranty repairs must be performed by a VistaTech-authorized service
center. Performing repairs at unauthorized locations voids the remaining
warranty. A list of authorized service centers is available at
vistatech.com/service-centers.

6.2 Parts and Labor
During the warranty period, all parts and labor costs for covered repairs
are provided free of charge. Customers are responsible for shipping the TV
to the service center; VistaTech will cover the return shipping.

6.3 Replacement Units
If a defective unit cannot be repaired within a reasonable timeframe,
VistaTech will replace it with:
  • The same model, if available in inventory
  • A model of equal or greater value if the original model is discontinued
  Replacement units carry the remainder of the original warranty term or
  90 days from the replacement date, whichever is longer.

6.4 Data and Settings
VistaTech is not responsible for any loss of data, app settings, or
configurations resulting from warranty service. Customers are advised to
note their preferences before submitting a unit for repair or replacement.

SECTION 7: OUT-OF-WARRANTY SERVICE

If your TV is out of warranty or the damage is not covered, VistaTech still
offers repair services at standard rates:

7.1 Diagnostic Fee
A non-refundable diagnostic fee of $49 applies to all out-of-warranty
service requests. This fee is credited toward the total repair cost if you
choose to proceed with the repair.

7.2 Repair Estimates
After diagnosis, you will receive a written repair estimate. You may accept
the repair, decline and receive your unit back, or request data recovery
assistance if the issue involves the TV's internal storage.

7.3 Common Out-of-Warranty Repair Costs (approximate)
  • Screen replacement (cracked): $199–$599 depending on panel size
  • Main board replacement: $149–$249
  • Power supply repair: $89–$149
  • HDMI board replacement: $99–$149
  • Speaker system repair: $69–$99

SECTION 8: FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: My TV has a few dead pixels. Is this covered?
A: Dead pixels are covered if there are 5 or more permanently non-functioning
   pixels within any 1cm² area. Isolated 1–4 dead pixels do not meet the
   coverage threshold under our Standard Warranty.

Q: My TV screen has burn-in from leaving a static image on too long. Is this covered?
A: Burn-in caused by user behavior (leaving static content displayed) is not
   covered under the Standard Warranty. However, Extended and Premium warranty
   holders may claim OLED image retention issues within the first 2 years of purchase.

Q: Can I upgrade my warranty after purchase?
A: Warranty upgrades are available within 30 days of original purchase only.
   After this window, no upgrade is available.

Q: My TV was damaged in a power surge. Is this covered?
A: Power surge damage is excluded from all warranty tiers. We strongly recommend
   connecting your TV through a surge protector. Your homeowner's or renter's
   insurance may cover such events.

Q: I bought the TV second-hand. Does the warranty transfer?
A: VistaTech warranties are non-transferable and apply only to the original
   purchaser. Second-hand buyers are not eligible for warranty service.

Q: My TV is 2 months out of warranty. Can I get an exception?
A: VistaTech may, at its sole discretion, offer goodwill service for units
   that are up to 60 days past warranty expiration and exhibit defects
   consistent with manufacturing faults. Contact our support team to request
   a goodwill assessment.

Q: What happens if VistaTech cannot repair my TV?
A: If a covered unit cannot be repaired within a reasonable timeframe
   (generally 21 business days), VistaTech will replace it with an equal
   or better model, or offer a prorated refund based on the remaining
   warranty period.

Q: Do I need to register my TV to activate the warranty?
A: Your warranty is valid from the date of purchase whether or not you
   register. However, registering your TV at vistatech.com/register
   speeds up the claims process and ensures you receive firmware update
   notifications.
